Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long does delivery take?

We typically deliver within 3-5 business days. However, delivery times may vary depending on your location and current logistics conditions.

2. Can I return or exchange a product?

Yes, you can return or exchange your product within 7 days of delivery. Make sure the item is unused and in original packaging.

3. How do I track my order?

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking link via email and SMS. You can use it to monitor the delivery progress.

4. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and cash on delivery (COD) in selected areas.

5. Is it safe to shop on My Shop?

Absolutely. We use SSL encryption and secure payment gateways to keep your information protected.
